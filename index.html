Thought Games

Synopsis

A chance to unleash your mind, free of intent and judgement, to explore topics bound and unbound, furthering perspectives and hopefully revealing the nature of thought.

Thought Games is an amateur-run meetup and organisation with the goal of facilitating open discussion on a range of topics, from the wildly absurd to the insanely quotidian. Examples of on-topic discussions would be:

  • The diplomatic implications of a nation-bound zombie apocalypse
  • The technological singularity and how it will affect humankind’s social nature
  • The ethical ramifications of uneaten toast in Britain
  • The practice of animal husbandry, framed as a moral dilemma
  • The human species, as viewed through the eyes of a type III civilization

Thought Games is considered by its creators as a “debating club designed for calm adults” discussing ideas of “utmost intrigue, hilarity and gravity”.

The meetups themselves are orchestrated through a series of concept games that drive participants towards new conceptual discoveries and formations.

What is not served by currently existing meetups?

To our eyes, there is nothing that facilitates discussions that are:

  • Unbound by the conventional academic or societal zeitgeist of our time
  • Flexible towards both hilarity and solemnity, on a variety of topics
  • At their core, non-confrontational and free of political, religious and societal impositions
